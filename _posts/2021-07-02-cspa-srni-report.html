The purpose of this short indicator assessment is to check the current skill levels of candidates in both Reading and Numeracy. Unlike the full CSPA assessments in Reading & Numeracy, the SRNI does not provide an ACSF* level estimate. Instead, the results provide an indication of whether a candidate is above or below ACSF Exit Level 2 in each of these key skills. Please note that candidates who have been assessed by SRNI as being above ACSF Exit Level 2 may well be performing at much higher levels of the ACSF.

Any candidates identified by the SRNI as below Exit Level 2 of the ACSF should undertake a more comprehensive assessment of their LLN skills using the full suite of CSPA assessments and potentially be targeted for specific LLN support programs.

*ACSF = Australian Core Skills Framework
